ABOUT COLLEGE:



MRIET believes that education is not just a mere act of teaching- learning, but should stretch beyond such narrow definition to inculcate sound human values, respect for tradition and an outlook that prepares individuals ready to fall challenges in life ahead. We believe to equip the students with leading capabilities to perform their best in technical fields. We emphasize on empowering them by providing training in Soft Skills and Personality Development which will enable them to succeed everywhere with a spirit of challenge and ambition. MRIET, the jewel in the crown of MRGI was established in 2007 with approval from AICTE, permanent affiliation to JNTUH and accredited by NBA. The institute offers B.Tech and M.Tech courses in CSE, IT, ECE and EEE branches and Post Graduate course MBA.
